Welcome

Hello, I’m Chelsea Conley and this website is all about my education and work experience. The purpose of this website is to showcase the versatility of my background and why I would be an asset to any team. My goal is for the website to give a clear picture of how I can be an asset to an employer in a wide range of positions. I will achieve this through two objectives: 1) to list at least 3 previous work positions that incorporate a description that focuses on the use of at least two of my degrees and 2) to detail in one paragraph how my degrees work together to make me a versatile employee.
